Problem Statement

Dry eye affects a huge portion of the population, yet the products designed to treat it are largely inconvenient, disposable, and fragmented. The goal was to design a single, portable system that consolidated multiple care solutions in one intuitive device — hands-free, customisable, and easy to use daily.

Design Targets

Portability · Ease of use · Hands-free operation · Customisability · Reusability over disposables

The Concept

One station. Three devices.

SCOPE acts as a wireless charging station for three separate eye care devices: a heated eye massager (SCOPE-A), a humidifier and eye drop spray (SCOPE-H), and a detachable sleeping mask stored in its backpack compartment.

Magnetic wireless charging slots make it effortless to dock and undock each device. The system is designed to live on a desk or bedside table — present enough to be used, unobtrusive enough not to be annoying.

SCOPE-A

Heated eye massager — detachable arms provide targeted warmth and relief.

SCOPE-H

Humidifier + eye drop spray — a removable heart module for moisture delivery.

Sleep Mask

Stored in SCOPE's backpack compartment, ready to deploy at night.

Smart System

Accompanied by a smartbot

SCOPE's screen displays weather, battery charge for each device, and blinks steadily to remind the user to take a screen break. The bot system turns a passive charging station into an active health companion.
